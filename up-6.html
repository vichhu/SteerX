Pros & Cons of Koenigsegg Gemera

Car Dekho Experts:

The Koenigsegg Gemera is the ultimate statement of power. It's the best family super car,
imposing, comfortable and is powered by a massive 5.0 L twin-turbocharged V8 petrol engine.
But, the waiting periods can stretch over 1 to 2 years!

 Things We Like


  • This big sports car has been a symbol of power, and the latest
    one continues to look imposing.

  • New interiors feel premium and classy, unlike other super cars
    which try to look futuristic and quirky.

  • The first 4-seater hyper car in the world has two turbos and drives the front wheels while also charging the batteries,it is rated at 440 kW at 7500 rpm with a redline at 8500 rpm and 600Nm of torque from 2000 to 7000 rpm
    more than enough for whatever use you may have.

  • Spacious, comfortable and feature loaded.

 Things We Don't Like


  • Waiting periods stretch in years

  • India does not get this variant

  • Full import, hence a hefty price tag
