Pros & Cons of Volvo C40 Recharge

Car Dekho Experts:

The Volvo C40 is the ultimate statement of power. It's big,
imposing, comfortable and is powered by a massive rechargable battery.
But, the waiting periods can stretch over 1 to 2 years!

 Things We Like


  • This big SUV has been a symbol of power, and the latest
    one continues to look imposing.

  • New interiors feel premium and classy, unlike other SUV
    which try to look futuristic and quirky.

  • The 78kWh battery pack has 660Nm of peak torque,
    more than enough for whatever use you may have.

  • Spacious, comfortable and feature loaded.

 Things We Don't Like


  • Waiting periods stretch in years

  • India only gets the 5-seater variant

  • Full import, hence a hefty price tag
