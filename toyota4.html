Pros & Cons of Toyota Fortuner Legender Car

SteerX Experts:

The Fortuner Facelift looks fresh and more premium than before,
while the updated feature list keeps it up to date with the
competition. The only concern now is the price, which has gone up
by upto Rs 3 lakh, and makes the Fortuner the costliest SUV in the
segment.

 Things We Like


  • More powerful diesel engine

  • 2021 facelift looks sportier than before

  • Legender looks different and more stylish than the regular
    Fortuner

  • Added features aid convenience in the cabin

  • Electronic Differential Lock will aid off-road ability

 Things We Don't Like


  • Still does not get a sunroof

  • Fortuner has become costlier by up to Rs 3 lakh

  • Legender does not get the 11-speaker music system
