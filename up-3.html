Pros & Cons of Aston Martin DB 12

Car Dekho Experts:

The Aston MArtin DB 12 is the ultimate statement of power.,
imposing, comfortable and is powered by a massive v8 engine.
But, the waiting periods can stretch over 1 to 2 years!

 Things We Like


  • This car has been a symbol of power, and the latest
    one continues to look imposing.

  • New interiors feel premium and classy, unlike other SUV
    which try to look futuristic and quirky.

  • The 4.0 litre M177 twin-turbo v8 enigine with 800Nm of torque,
    more than enough for whatever use you may have.
